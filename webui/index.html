How Does This Work?

As a Data Scientist, you need to use managed Jupyter Notebooks. You also often search for data for your experiment, and wish you could find some code available that will allow to do build the best possible decision model with that data. With this app, once you sign up, you can browse thrpigh our catalog of hosted data and accompanying notebooks. Once you find the one you like, you do one click provision of execution of the code.
